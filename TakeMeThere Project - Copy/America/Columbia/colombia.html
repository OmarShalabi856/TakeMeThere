COLOMBIA



General Information :

Colombia, officially Republic of Colombia, Spanish República de Colombia, country of northwestern South America. Its 1,000 miles (1,600 km) of coast to the north are bathed by the waters of the Caribbean Sea, and its 800 miles (1,300 km) of coast to the west are washed by the Pacific Ocean.

More About Colombia

  • Temperature and Climate: Most parts of Colombia are affected by tropical climates, this is characterized by quite high temperatures throughout the year and fairly high amounts of rainfall, especially along the western coastal areas..
  • Food: food of Colombia is very diverse! Famous traditional Colombian food includes:Arrepas,Bandeja Paisa,Ajiaco,Lechona,Sancocho,Chicharron and many more
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 26$/Day on average
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: Leaning Rosario Islands,Valle del Cocora,Catedral del sal,Monserrate.
