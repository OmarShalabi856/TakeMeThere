General Information :

The crown jewel of the middle east , Turkey is a great afordable destination for those who want a new experience , filled with history and culture ,Turkey is surely a testament to the beauty of this world.

More About Turkey

  • Temperature and Climate: Turkey has an absolutely perfect climate, as it has snowy winters and moderate to hot summers, with all 4 seasons in between.
  • Food: Famous eastern cuisine such as: Yaprak dolma, Inegol kofte, Cag Kebab, Shwarmas, and Turkish Icecream .
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 45$/Day on average
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: Galata Tower, Hagia Sophia Mosque, Topkapi Palace Museum.
