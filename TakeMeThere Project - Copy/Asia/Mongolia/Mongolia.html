MONGOLIA



General Information :

From the endless mountains and stunning scenery , to the history behind it, once an empire ,Mongolia is a great destination for couples who seek stories to hear , adventures to make ,and go to breathtaking sights.

Photos Of Mongolia

More About Mongolia

  • Temperature and Climate: Known as one of the coldest in the area, Mongolia experiences very cold winters, with short summers.
  • Food: Unique dishes such as: Huushuur, Bansh, and Chicken Makh.
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 20$/Day on average
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: Gorkhi Terelj National Park, Gandantegchinlen Monastery, Chinggis Khaan Statue.
