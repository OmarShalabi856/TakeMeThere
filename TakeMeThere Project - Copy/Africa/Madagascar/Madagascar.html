MADAGASCAR



General Information :

Madagascar is an island located in the bottom east of Africa.The geography of Madagascar can be divided into five regions: the East Coast, the Central Highlands, the Tsaratanana Massif, the West Coast and the Southwest. It is also known of being home of lemurs colorful chameleons, stunning orchids, and towering baobab trees
