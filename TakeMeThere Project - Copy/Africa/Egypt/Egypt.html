Egypt



General Information :

Egypt is located in northern Africa. It occupies a strategic location on the African continent. It extends from the Mediterranean Sea in the north to Sudan in the south, and from the Red Sea in the east to Libyan Arab Jamahiriya in the west.People of egypt are well known for there humbleness , generosity and sense of humor. With a population at about 100.4 million, Egypt ranks 14th by world population.

More About Egypt

  • Temperature and Climate: Egypt is characterized by a Semi desert climate.Summers are usually hot and dry, and winters are moderate with very little rain.Temperatures are around (9.5-21°C) in winter. As for the summer, temperatures are usually high ranging from 20 to 30 degrees .
  • Food: The Egyption cuisine is one of the most famous cuisines in the middle east! Famous traditional egyption food includes: Kushari, Fool Mdammas , Hamam Mahshi, Fool w Taameya, and many more
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 25$/Day on average
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: Pyramids of Giza, Nile River, Karnak Temple
