ETHIOPIA



General Information :

Ethiopia is located in the Horn of Africa. It is bordered by Eritrea to the north, Djibouti and Somalia to the east, Sudan and South Sudan to the west, and Kenya to the south. Ethiopia is known for its numerous natural wonders. Ethiopia is also the home of some of the world's most beautiful landscapes. The capital Addis Ababa is famous for its stunning scenary and great food.

More About Ethiopia

  • Temperature and Climate: Ethiopia has different climate zones including tropical savanna (west) and warm dessert climate in the east.Temperatures are ideal September-November (25˚C).Ethiopia is generally warm, but some areas such as Omo River Region or Red Sea are extreme (60 ˚C)
  • Food: The Ethiopian cuisine consists of vegetable and often very spicy meat dishes. Ethiopian food expresses its culture that food is shared with friends and family.Famous traditional dishes include: Tibs, Kitfo, Fuul, Tere Siga and many more!
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 29$/Day on average
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: Blue Nile Falls, Erta Ale, Simien Mountains, Addis Ababa.
