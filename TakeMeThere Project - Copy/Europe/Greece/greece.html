GREECE



General Information :

Greece is a country located in Southeast Europe. Its population is approximately 10.7 million.
Athens is its largest and capital city, followed by Thessaloniki. Situated on the southern tip of the Balkans, Greece is located at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa.
Greece is one of those rare places where ancient history is still very much a part of modern life. Visitors are as much drawn to the country’s epic intellectual history as its beauty and charm.
Greece has around 2000 islands, for a peaceful beach vacation, the island of Santorini is perfect.

More About Greece

  • Temperature and Climate: Greece has a Mediterranean climate with hot and dry summers and sometimes cold and wet winters.
    Temperatures average 29°C in August but can exceed 38°C .
    Its capaital Athens is the hottest capital city in Europe.
  • Food: The Greek cuisine is part of the Mediterranean cuisine, influenced by various Middle Eastern cuisines.
    Famous traditional Greek food includes: Gyro, Kleftiko, Keftethes, Papoutsakia, Pastitsio.
  • Average Amount Of Money Needed: 50$/Day on average.
  • Top Rated Tourist Attractions: The Acropolis, Santorini island, The Parthenon, The Temple of Olympian Zeus.
